James Harden

About James Harden

James Harden is a professional basketball player. He currently plays for the Houston Rockets and primarily plays both guard positions. After graduating from Arizona State University, James was drafted by the Oklahoma City Thunder. Harden won Sixth Man of the Year in 2012 playing for the Thunder. At the end of the 2012 season, Harden was traded to the Rockets where he began to emerge as a superstar. Harden since then has won an MVP, two scoring titles, and has become the face of Adidas basketball. Recently, James Harden signed a 4-year fully guaranteed contract for $171,131,520 with the Houston Rockets.
